Revision Policy

At Maestro Essay, we are committed to ensuring your complete satisfaction with the academic writing services we provide. Our revision policy allows you to request modifications to your delivered work to meet your expectations, provided the requests align with the original instructions.

Free Revisions

We offer free revisions within 14 days of the delivery of your order, subject to the following conditions:

  • Revisions must be based on the original order instructions provided at the time of placing the order.
  • Requests for revisions must be submitted within 14 days from the date the completed order is delivered to you.
  • Revisions are limited to adjustments that improve the quality of the work, such as correcting errors, clarifying content, or addressing minor formatting issues.

Exclusions

The following scenarios are not covered under our free revision policy:

  • Requests that significantly alter the original instructions, such as changing the topic, academic level, or scope of the work.
  • Revisions requested after the 14-day revision period has expired.
  • Additional work that was not part of the original order, such as adding new sections or increasing the page count.

How to Request a Revision

To request a revision, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Maestro Essay account.
  2. Navigate to the order in question and select the "Request Revision" option.
  3. Provide detailed instructions for the changes needed, referencing the original order requirements.
  4. Submit your request, and our team will review it within 24 hours.

We aim to complete revision requests within 2-5 business days, depending on the complexity of the changes. Urgent revisions may be accommodated upon request, subject to availability.

Our Commitment

If a revision request is valid and aligns with the original instructions, we will assign the task to the original writer whenever possible to ensure consistency. Our goal is to deliver a final product that meets your academic needs and adheres to the highest standards of quality.

For any questions about our revision policy, please contact our support team at support@maestroessay.com or via our 24/7 live chat.
